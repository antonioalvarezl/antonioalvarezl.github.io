Bio

I am a PhD student at Universidad Autónoma de Madrid (affiliated with Instituto de Ciencias Matemáticas) and Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg. My research focuses on the mathematical foundations of machine learning, specifically on the study of controlled flows for supervised learning and generative modeling.

Currently, I am writing my thesis while visiting my PhD advisor Prof. Enrique Zuazua at Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg, following a fruitful research stay at the National University of Singapore (August – November 2025), where I worked in the team of Prof. Qianxiao Li.

Bio

I am a researcher in the mathematical foundations of machine learning. I recently completed my PhD at Universidad Autónoma de Madrid , affiliated with the Instituto de Ciencias Matemáticas , in collaboration with Friedrich-Alexander-Universität Erlangen-Nürnberg .

My research focuses on controlled dynamical systems for machine learning, with particular interest in supervised learning, generative modeling, neural differential equations, and the mathematical foundations of deep learning.

    New preprint on arXiv! In collaboration with Lorenzo Liverani and Enrique Zuazua, we uploaded our work "Constructive interpolation and generalization rates for neural ODEs: a control perspective" .

    We constructively prove exact interpolation for semi-autonomous neural ODEs, obtain nonparametric population-risk rates with estimates on the required width, and compare with purely autonomous models.
